About Us

Abelemkpe, Accra

Dimaensa Restaurant is a welcoming dining space located in Abelemkpe, Accra. Our name comes from a popular Ghanaian expression that means ‘eat till the last drop’ — a reminder of the joy great food brings.

We blend traditional Ghanaian dishes with warm hospitality, creating a homely environment for friends, families, and food lovers. Whether you’re stopping by for lunch, dinner, or a relaxed evening meal, Dimaensa offers a true taste of Ghana.
